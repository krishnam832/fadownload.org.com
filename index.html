What is LibreOffice?

LibreOffice is a free and open-source office suite that offers a variety of applications for document creation, data processing, and presentation design. It is a powerful alternative to proprietary office software, offering compatibility with many document formats.

Features

  • Writer – Word Processor
  • Calc – Spreadsheet Editor
  • Impress – Presentation Software
  • Draw – Vector Graphics Editor
  • Base – Database Management
  • Math – Formula Editor
